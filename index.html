Welcome to KTJ Logistics, LLC

KTJ Logistics is a trusted, woman-owned trucking company based in Nashville, Tennessee. Since 2020, we’ve provided reliable dry van transportation for palletized freight across the Southeastern United States.

We proudly operate as a business-to-business (B2B) carrier, working only with established companies and freight brokers. Owned and operated by Tamika Owens, KTJ Logistics delivers professional, punctual service on every load.
